AN/USM-128

The AN/USM-128 is a portable test set for pre-flight check-out of electrical power systems of aircraft and ground support power equipment. The AN/USM-128 provides measurement of frequency, A.C. and D.C. voltages and phase rotation.

Features:

  • Simple operation
  • Simple design
  • Strong durability under extreme temperatures, shock resistant, water resistant, radio interference, etc.
  • Government conformed materials
