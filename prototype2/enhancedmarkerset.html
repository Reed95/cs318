Enhanced Marker Set

The Enhanced NBC Marker Set is an improved version of the standard marking set currently used by the Army's M93 and M93A1 Fox Nuclear, Biological and Chemical Reconnaissance System (NBCRS). Our objective was to provide a more effective means of identifying areas of Nuclear, Biological and Chemical contamination that would pose hazards to advancing ground troops.
