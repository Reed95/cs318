F-16 Adaptor Kit

The fuel adapter FR216D0100-1 is used in certifying external aircraft fuel tanks on the A-7 Corsair, F-15 Eagle and F-16 Falcon fighter jets. Test box assembly

What's in the kit?

  • Test box assembly
  • Air adaptor assembly
  • Fuel adaptor assembly
  • Adaptor kit assembly
  • Wiring harnesses (x4)
    		•

    Specifications:

    Air and fuel adapters
  • Adapter kit permits certification of the 300 gallon & 370 gallon tanks for the aircraft
  • Quick disconnect wiring harnesses
  • ISO9001:2008 (Quality Management System)
  • Meets Specifications:
    • Mil-B-5423
    • Mil-C-26482
    • Mil-C-5541
    • Mil-C83723
    • Mil-T-21200K
  • Environment resistant
  • Harnesses are fabricated to meet Mil-W-5088 specification
